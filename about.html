Our history

Showtag Enabled is a is a powerful platform that helps make your videos come to life. Enable your content be more engaging in an unobtrusive manner. Showtag Enabled lets you to tag products, people and music. We let you incorporate forms, surveys, quizzes, insert banners, menus and such. Educate the audience beyond more that what's in the main video itself. And yes,the possibilities are endless.

Careers

Tag designer
(part-time)

Oslo, Norway

A tag designer creates the look, layout, and features of the interface embedded in the video player. The job involves understanding both graphic design and computer programming. A must have are excellent skills in CSS, HTML5 and PhotoShop preferred with knowledge of CSS animation. Your work will mostly revolve around the design of the next generation engagement experience in embedded streaming and mobile video. You will work closely with talented accountmanagers, engineers and product managers while engaging internal stakeholders. The ideal candidate for this role will be able to translate high-level strategy into great designs. What will you do? Sketch out rough interactions and interfaces to help define new features and ideas. – Iterate on these ideas and build clickable prototypes that can be used for internal reviews and tests – Help shape the design culture at Showtag that will ultimately define every interaction customers have with our products and services.

Skills:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Base64, SVG

We can offer you an international working environment filled with smart and ambitious colleagues. We know that diverse teams are strong teams, so we welcome those from different backgrounds and experiences.
